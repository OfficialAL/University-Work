2 Dollar Chicken Curry


Supplies Needed:
Induction Cooktop
Hand Blender

Step 1: Prepare Ingredients


Chicken
1 Cup (240g) Full-Fat Yoghurt
1 Tbsp (15g) garam Masala
1 Tbsp (15g) Salt
1 1/2 lbs (680g) Boneless, Skinless chicken thighs

Butter Chicken Curry
2 Tbsp (30g) vegetable Oil
2-inch knob of ginger, finely diced
6 cloves of sliced garlic
Salt and Pepper to taste
2 tsp (8g) Paprika
2 tsp (8g) Ground Cumin
1 Tbsp (14g) Garam Masala
1 1/2 tsp (6g) Tumeric Powder (Optional)
14oz Crushed Tomatoes
2/3 cup (160ml) Water
1 cup (240ml) Heavy Cream
2 Tbsp (28g) Unsalted Butter
Steamed Rice
Fresh coriander

Method:

Step 1

In a medium bowl, whisk together yogurt, garam masala, and salt. Explain the second step of the recipe.

Step 2

Toss with sliced chicken thighs until fully coated. Cover and marinate for 30 minutes or overnight.Describe the first step of the cooking process.

Step 3

In a large pan, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat until shimmering hot. Explain the second step of the recipe.

Step 4

Add chicken in batches and sear 2-3 minutes on each side. Set to side once done.Explain the second step of the recipe.

Step 5

Lower the same pan to medium heat, add a touch of more oil along with diced onions, diced ginger, sliced garlic, and season to taste with salt and pepper. Mix and stir, sauteing for 2-3 minutes or until softened.

Step 6

Add in spices (turmeric powder optional) and stir together until spices have toasted for 1-2 minutes.

Step 7

After toasting, add in crushed tomatoes and water then stir until incorporated over medium heat. Let simmer for 5-8 minutes until the sauce has reduced. Explain the second step of the recipe.

Step 8

Once reduced, add chicken back to the pan mixing into the sauce and let simmer for 3-5 minutes.Describe the first step of the cooking process.

Step 9

Add in heavy cream stirring until thickened and incorporated. Bring up to a light simmer before reducing heat to low and simmering for no longer than 3-4 minutes. Stir often. Explain the second step of the recipe.

Step 10

Turn off the heat and continuously stir in unsalted butter until melted. Season to taste with salt. Explain the second step of the recipe.

Step 11

Plate in a shallow bowl with steam rice and topped with corriander.
